Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Advanced Clock ("the app") is a home screen clock and date widget for Android. This page explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data Collection

Advanced Clock does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app has no analytics, no advertising SDK, no crash reporting service, and no network communication of any kind. All settings you configure (time zone, date format, colour, layout) are stored exclusively on your device using Android's local DataStore and are never shared with anyone.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM — used to schedule precise one-minute widget refresh ticks so the displayed time stays accurate. No alarm data is read or transmitted.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — used to restore the widget after the device restarts. No data is collected during this process.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect data.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
